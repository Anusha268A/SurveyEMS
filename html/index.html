Welcome Residents

Welcome to the Society Survey & Representative Election Management System. This platform enables residents to participate in surveys, share valuable feedback, and vote for society representatives in a transparent and efficient manner.

Purpose of the System

The purpose of this system is to improve communication between residents and society management. Residents can express their opinions through surveys, report issues, and participate in elections to choose responsible representatives for the welfare of the community.

Election Announcement

The Annual Society Representative Election 2026 will be conducted on 15 June 2026. Eligible residents are encouraged to review candidate profiles, participate in discussions, and cast their votes before the election deadline.
